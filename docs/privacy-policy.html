Privacy Policy

Effective July 22, 2026

The short version

Labophase does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data you enter stays on your device.

Overview

About This Policy

Labophase ("the app") is a tournament-tracking tool for the One Piece Card Game. This privacy policy describes our data practices — which are, simply put, that we handle none of your personal information.

Data Collection

Information We Do Not Collect

Labophase does not collect, request, or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial or payment information

No account is required to use the app.

Local Storage

Data Stored on Your Device

Tournament entries, round results, and leader selections you record in Labophase are stored locally on your device using Android's built-in database (SQLite). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Labophase does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

Children

Children's Privacy

Labophase does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Because the app collects no personal data at all, it presents no privacy risk to users of any age.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at commanderpepperdev@gmail.com.
